Why you should come to User Group Connect

  • Show your flag! Let people know about your events
  • New people will find out about you and go to your meetings
  • Find new people who might speak at your meetings
  • Find other groups that are similar to yours. Example:
    • Agile Ottawa and SCRUM user groups share a Christmas meeting
    • They also share (a few) members and speakers

Suggestions for what to present

  • bring a demonstration of what you do (eg robotics) and have someone working on it and explaining it
  • if the topic you're interested in can be found in everyday life, bring some examples (eg, Linux in Samsung TVs, ham radio used in real life emergencies)
  • give a talk on a related topic (possibly by an invited famous speaker)
  • give away CD's or other material to promote your group
  • be sure to have handouts or business cards with your meeting times and locations
  • other handouts like CDs with software or pictures/video, or stickers is sure to be well-received (and remembered and passed on) by visitors
